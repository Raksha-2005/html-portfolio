BEST SERIES I HAVE EVER WATCHED

My top 3 series

The Sound Of Magic

My first ever series and the most memorable one.It beautifully depicts the friendship between two people who have extremely different preception of life.
Genre:Fantasy,mystery,Psychological

Twinkling Watermelon

Time travel based series where the male lead tries to change the fate of his deaf parents but he coundn't change as he wished but there were unexpected changes waiting for him in the future
Genre:Comedy,Time travel,Piece of life

Silent Sea

One of the interesting series I have ever watched.A crew is send to moon to find the confidential capsule which is very essential to reproduce water again on earth.There they find out many buried secrets and other illegal actions the space company was doing so far.
